Privacy Policy for LeetCommit

Effective Date: May 18, 2025

LeetCommit is a browser extension that helps users automatically push their LeetCode submissions to GitHub. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect and how we handle it.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.
  • We do not access or store your LeetCode credentials.
  • We do not track user behavior or usage data.

2. GitHub Authentication

LeetCommit uses your personal GitHub token solely for committing code to the repository you specify. This token is stored securely in your browser’s local storage and is never transmitted to any external servers.

3. Third-Party Services

LeetCommit does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or data tracking services.

4. Data Storage

All configuration and data remain on your local machine. No external data storage or cloud services are used.

5. Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this policy, please contact us via the GitHub repo’s Issues page or at your.email@example.com.

By using LeetCommit, you agree to this privacy policy. This policy may be updated periodically, and changes will be reflected on this page.
